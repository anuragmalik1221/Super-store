# Super Store Sales Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project features an interactive **Microsoft Excel dashboard** built using the Super Store dataset. It leverages Excel tools such as formulas, PivotTables, PivotCharts, and data visualization techniques to analyze sales performance and business trends. In addition to the dashboard, a separate worksheet presents a detailed report highlighting key business insights and actionable recommendations.

## Business Objective

Identify **three key findings** and **three strategic recommendations** to help increase sales for the Super Store.

## Workbook Structure

* **Dashboard (Sheet 1):** An interactive dashboard displaying key performance metrics, charts, and visualizations.
* **Report (Sheet 2):** A summary of the key findings and recommended business actions.
* **Supporting Sheets:** Additional data and analysis referenced in the report.

## Report

### Key Findings

1. **Excessive Discounts on Binders**

   * Binders receive significant discounts despite already being one of the highest-selling sub-categories across all customer segments and regions.

2. **Uneven Discount Distribution**

   * A large portion of the discount budget is allocated to stores in the Central region, while stores in the Southern region receive comparatively fewer discounts.

3. **Low Average Basket Size**

   * Approximately **50.65%** of all orders contain only a single product, indicating an opportunity to increase cross-selling and average order value.

4. **Slower Deliveries in the Central Region**

   * Home Office customers in the Central region experience an average delivery time that is **0.21 days longer** than the overall average for the same customer segment.

### Recommendations

1. **Optimize the Discount Strategy**

   * Reduce discounts on Binders—or Office Supplies in general—since they already perform well. Reallocate part of the discount budget to stores in the Southern region to stimulate demand and improve regional sales.

2. **Increase Cross-Selling Opportunities**

   * For online sales, implement machine learning–based product recommendation systems to suggest complementary products and encourage customers purchasing a single item to add related products to their carts.
   * For physical stores, improve product placement by adopting effective **planogram** strategies to increase product visibility and encourage additional purchases.

3. **Improve Delivery Performance**

   * Reduce delivery times in the Central region by upgrading shipping methods wherever feasible. Faster deliveries can improve customer satisfaction and contribute to increased sales.
